How it works

  • Drag images anywhere to auto-fill; drag a filled cell to move/swap; drag off the page to delete.
  • To duplicate, hold D while dragging and drop on the target cell.
  • Double-click a cell to replace its image.
  • Paste screenshots or copied images (Ctrl/‚åò+V) to auto-fill empty cells, or focus a cell first to target it.
  • New pages are created automatically when needed.
  • Print prints all pages exact size.

Image fit: Cover vs Contain

  • Cover ‚Äî Scales the image so the cell is completely filled; extra edges may be cropped. Best for edge-to-edge proxies without letterboxing.
  • Contain ‚Äî Fits the whole image inside the cell; nothing is cropped, so you may see empty margins (‚Äúletterboxing‚Äù).
