Help & Hotkeys

Hotkeys

  • Arrow keys – Move focus between cells
  • Ctrl/⌘+← / Ctrl/⌘+→ – Prev/Next page ([/] also works)
  • Home – First page (Fn+← on Mac)
  • End – Last page (Fn+→ on Mac)
  • N – Add page
  • Shift+Delete/Backspace – Remove current page (confirm)
  • Ctrl/⌘+Delete/Backspace – Clear current page
  • Ctrl/⌘+Shift+Delete/Backspace – Clear ALL pages
  • Alt+Arrows – Reorder focused cell
  • D + Drag – Duplicate dragged card to target cell (keeps original)
  • Delete/Backspace – Clear focused cell
  • Ctrl/⌘+C – Copy focused cell image
  • Ctrl/⌘+V – Paste images from your clipboard
  • 1–9 – Focus a cell
  • Enter in card field – Add card
  • P – Print all pages
  • G – Toggle guides
  • M – Toggle cut marks
  • ? or H or F1 – Open this help
  • Esc – Close any open dialog

How it works

  • Drag images anywhere to auto-fill; drag a filled cell to move/swap; drag off the page to delete.
  • To duplicate, hold D while dragging and drop on the target cell.
  • Double-click a cell to replace its image.
  • Paste screenshots or copied images (Ctrl/⌘+V) to auto-fill empty cells, or focus a cell first to target it.
  • New pages are created automatically when needed.
  • Print prints all pages exact size.

Image fit: Cover vs Contain

  • Cover — Scales the image so the cell is completely filled; extra edges may be cropped. Best for edge-to-edge proxies without letterboxing.
  • Contain — Fits the whole image inside the cell; nothing is cropped, so you may see empty margins (“letterboxing”).

Decklist importer

  • Paste one card per line (e.g., 4 Lightning Bolt). You can include a set code like [M11] or (M11).
  • Double-faced cards use the front face (enable “Include DFC back faces” to add the reverse).
  • Use the checkboxes to skip basics and Snow-Covered basics.
  • Images are fetched via Scryfall and placed across pages automatically; unknown cards are skipped.

* “Through the Omenpaths” cards are currently not compatible.

⚠️ Right-click copy/paste is limited in the browser version. Use the desktop app for full clipboard support.
